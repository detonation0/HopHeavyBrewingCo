About Us

Over the past few years, we have developed a passion for brewing craft beer. In August of 2014, we decided it was time to build a brewery and make a name for ourselves. That's when Hop Heavy came to life. With the help of family and friends the brewery construction is now complete. It is located inside the back half of our garage. The entire build took just under a year, starting with the framing of the actual room. To see the entire process of our build, please check out our page on Facebook here.
